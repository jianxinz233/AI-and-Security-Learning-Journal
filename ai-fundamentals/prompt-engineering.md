# Prompt Engineering

## What I Learned

Prompt engineering is the practice of writing clear and structured instructions for AI systems to improve the quality and usefulness of their responses.

A good prompt often includes:

- context
- task
- constraints
- expected output format

## Why It Matters

In business environments, prompt quality can affect the usefulness, accuracy, and safety of AI-generated output.

Poor prompts may lead to vague, incomplete, or misleading responses.

## Example

Instead of:

> Write a summary.

A more structured prompt could be:

> Summarize this customer feedback in three bullet points. Focus on recurring issues, possible business impact, and suggested next actions.

Providing clear instructions and context usually produces more useful results.

## 🧶 Personal Example: Knitting Pattern Assistance

I noticed that prompt quality also affects how useful AI can be in hobby and planning workflows.

For example, when asking for help adapting a knitting pattern, simply asking:

> Can I use another yarn for this pattern?

usually produces very general suggestions.

A more effective workflow is first understanding the pattern structure and identifying the actual decision points before writing the prompt.

For example:

> I have a knitting pattern written for Drops Flora + Kid Silk held together with a target gauge of 18 stitches per 10 cm on 5 mm needles.
>
> I want to substitute the yarn using Holst Supersoft held single.
>
> My measured gauge with 4.5 mm needles is 20 stitches per 10 cm after washing.
>
> The pattern is designed with positive ease.
>
> Can you help evaluate:
>
> - whether this yarn substitution is realistic,
> - whether needle size adjustments may help,
> - and whether I may need to adjust sizing based on the new gauge?

I realized that AI responses become much more practical when I:

- first understand the general pattern structure,
- identify the real problem I am trying to solve,
- provide measurements and constraints,
- and ask specific questions instead of broad ones.

This also creates a smoother workflow because the AI can support decision-making more effectively instead of only giving generic guidelines.

## Reflection

Prompt engineering is not only a technical skill. It is also connected to communication, business understanding, structured thinking, and clearly defining constraints.
