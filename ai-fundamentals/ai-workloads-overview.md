# AI Workloads Overview

## What I Learned

AI systems can support different types of tasks depending on the problem being solved.

Some common AI workloads include:

- machine learning
- computer vision
- natural language processing (NLP)
- conversational AI
- generative AI

## Examples

### Machine Learning

Machine learning can identify patterns in data and make predictions or classifications.

Examples:

- fraud detection
- sales forecasting
- recommendation systems

### Computer Vision

Computer vision allows systems to interpret images or video.

Examples:

- facial recognition
- object detection
- quality inspection in manufacturing

### Natural Language Processing (NLP)

NLP focuses on understanding and processing human language.

Examples:

- sentiment analysis
- translation
- keyword extraction

### Conversational AI

Conversational AI enables interaction through chatbots or virtual assistants.

Examples:

- customer support chatbots
- internal knowledge assistants

### Generative AI

Generative AI can create new content such as text, images, or code.

Examples:

- AI writing assistants
- image generation
- code suggestions

## Reflection

One thing I found interesting is that many AI systems used in business environments are not fully autonomous systems, but tools that support decision-making, communication, or workflow efficiency.

This also helped me understand why governance, human review, and responsible AI practices are important alongside technical capabilities.
