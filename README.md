# AI and Security Learning Journal

This repository documents my structured learning journey in AI, cybersecurity foundations, and technology governance.

The goal is not to present myself as an expert, but to show how I explore emerging technology topics in a structured, practical, and reflective way.

## Focus Areas

- AI fundamentals
- Cybersecurity foundations
- Governance, risk, and responsible technology use
- Small practical exercises and reflections

## Why I Started This Project

I am interested in how AI and digital technologies are changing business workflows, customer communication, and operational processes.

With my background in communication, project coordination, and data analytics, I want to strengthen my technical awareness and better understand how technology can be applied responsibly in enterprise environments.
